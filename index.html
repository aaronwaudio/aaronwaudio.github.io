Instructions

Thanks for using my Transmutation Calculator! If you are looking to maximize your crafting potential, this tool is incredibly useful. Enter the amount of each tier of resource you have, and it will tell you how many to transmute so you are just transmuting the extras. You will have to run this several times to get all your resources into equilibrium. You can use the dropdown menu to choose which tiers you are transmuting. The amount field at the bottom tells you how many of the higher tier resources you should craft. That should give you exactly what you need for the maximum amount of resources. Enjoy!
