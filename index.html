Albion Online Resource Transmutation Calculator

Thanks for using my Transmutation Calculator! If you are looking to maximize your crafting potential, this tool is incredibly useful. Enter the amount of each tier of resource you have, and it will tell you how many to transmute so you are just transmuting the extras. I have found it is best to start with your highest transmutable resource first, since the change in amounts will trickle down and affect every other transmutation. You can use the dropdown menu to choose to transmute from tier 2 to tier 3, or any other higher tier transmutation. The amount field at the bottom tells you how many of the higher tier resources you should craft. That should give you exactly what you need for the maximum amount of resources. Enjoy!
